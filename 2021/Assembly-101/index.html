The most you can carry out of a single-column addition of 2 numbers is 1

Base 8 (Octal)

Octal
1 = 80
10 = 81
100 = 82
1000 = 83

Base 8 – Base 10

76225 (base 8)
= 80 * 5
+ 81 * 2
+ 82 * 2
+ 83 * 6
+ 84 * 7
= 31893 (base 10)


Base 16 (Hex)

Base 16 – Base 10

8DB3H (base 16)
= 160 * 3
+ 161 * 11
+ 162 * 13
+ 163 * 8
= 36275 (base 10)


Base 10 – Base 16 (余数法, next smallest number)

413 (base 10)
= 162 * 1
+ 161 * 9
+ 160 * 13
= 19DH (base 16)
